## Concepts

To be honest, the concepts are so easy and beginner-friendly.

- Step 1: Convert two images into their pixels (First image is the source and Second image is the target image)
- Step 2: Calculate brightness for each pixel, which is, just the average of RED, GREEN and BLUE of each pixel.
- Step 3: Then, Sort the array/image based on brightness.
- Step 4: Use those sorted image, and match pixels of both images.
- Step 5: With those pixels from both images, take colour from Image ONE and structure (that is, x and y coordinates) from image TWO.
- Step 6: Draw a new image with new pixels data. 
- Step 7: You could add Animations or optimize the algorithm by introducing range.
